# TRS-80_TC-8 Cassette Folder

Contains recordings from cassette related to the TC-8 system

## Overview

This folder will be where various recordings related to the TC-8 cassette system will
be compiled.

System-related programs, intended to enable functionality of the TC-8 system, will
in most cases be in the native Model I Level II 500 baud format, as they are intended
to be loaded at startup, in order to use the system.

Other programs/files may be recorded in the TC-8 2900 baud format for various reasons.

### WAV files

As you may expect, these are audio recordings made from actual tapes from the 1980s, and
are unlikely to have been processed in any way.  They may be 500 baud recordings from
Level II system, or may be 2900 baud made from the TC-8.

### CAS files

These are processed files, from 500 baud Level II programs, which are loadable into emulators.
These will have been processed by the "WAV2CAS" program by Knut Roll-Lund (also included
in this folder).
